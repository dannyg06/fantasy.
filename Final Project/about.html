About Freighter Bite

I'm Freighter Bite, the unapologetic fusion of creativity and rebellion. Confident, charismatic and unafraid to take risks, I embody the perfect blend of edge and elegance. Music and art are my passions, my therapies and my weapons. When I'm not crafting melodic mayhem or visual masterpieces, you can find me pushing boundaries and defying expectations.

Ethan Blackwood

Ethan Blackwood grew up in a small, coastal town, where he spent most of his childhood tinkering with electronics and teaching himself to code. As a shy and introverted teenager, Ethan found solace in music, spending hours in his makeshift home studio, experimenting with beats and melodies. After completing high school, Ethan enrolled in a local college, studying computer science and music production. However, it wasn't until he stumbled upon an underground electronic music scene that he discovered his true calling – and the persona of Freighter Bite was born.

Who I am?

"Growing up, I was always fascinated by the power of music and storytelling. As a kid, I would spend hours watching music videos and concerts, mesmerized by the performers' energy and charisma. But it wasn't until I discovered my own passion for singing and songwriting that I knew I wanted to pursue a career in music. I started writing my own songs and performing at local venues, and soon discovered that music was not only a way for me to express myself, but also a way to connect with others. As I continued to hone my craft, I began to explore other creative outlets, including YouTube and social media. I discovered that I loved creating content and sharing my ideas with others, and soon built a community of like-minded individuals who shared my passions. Today, as Freight Bite, I'm proud to be a singer, YouTuber, influencer, and vlogger, using my platforms to inspire and entertain others. I'm grateful for the opportunity to share my story and connect with all of you."

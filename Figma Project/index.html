Supernova

Supernovas are colossal stellar explosions that illuminate the cosmos, briefly outshining entire galaxies. These cataclysmic events occur when massive stars collapse, unleashing unfathomable energy and scattering elements forged within their cores into space. Supernovas play a pivotal role in shaping the universe, dispersing heavy elements necessary for planetary formation and life.
